Muzan Kibutsuji (Kimetsu no Yaiba)

Muzan Kibutsuji is the main antagonist of the anime series Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a powerful demon who has lived for centuries, feared by both humans and demons alike. Muzan is responsible for creating the Twelve Kizuki and turning Tanjiro Kamado's family into demons. He is a cunning and ruthless foe, using his powers to manipulate and control others to achieve his goals.
