Uchiha Madara (Naruto)

Uchiha Madara is a legendary shinobi from the anime series Naruto. He is one of the founding members of the Uchiha clan and a former leader of the clan. Madara is known for his immense power and skill in battle, as well as his mastery of the Sharingan and Rinnegan. He is considered one of the most powerful shinobi in history, capable of taking on entire armies single-handedly. Madara is a complex character with a tragic past, driven by a desire for power and revenge against the Hidden Leaf Village.
