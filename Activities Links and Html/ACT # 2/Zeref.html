Zeref (Fairy Tail)

Zeref Dragneel is a character from the anime series Fairy Tail. He is one of the main antagonists of the series, known as the Black Wizard. Zeref is a powerful dark mage with the ability to control life and death, and he is feared by many for his immense power. He is a complex character with a tragic past, driven by a desire to end his own life and bring about the destruction of the world. Zeref is a formidable opponent, capable of taking on multiple enemies at once and using his dark magic to crush his foes.
