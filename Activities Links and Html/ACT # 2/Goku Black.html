Goku Black (Dragon Ball Super)

Goku Black is a character from the anime series Dragon Ball Super. He is an alternate version of Goku from a different timeline, who has become a powerful villain known as Zamasu. Goku Black is a formidable foe, using his immense power and abilities to challenge Goku and his allies. He is a ruthless and cunning adversary, with a goal of eradicating all mortals and creating a new world in his image.
