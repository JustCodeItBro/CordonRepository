Toga Himiko (My Hero Academia)

Toga Himiko is a villain from the anime series My Hero Academia. She is a member of the League of Villains and a student at U.A. High School. Toga is known for her bloodlust and obsession with the hero Stain, as well as her ability to transform into other people by ingesting their blood. She is a dangerous and unpredictable foe, using her powers to deceive and manipulate her enemies.
