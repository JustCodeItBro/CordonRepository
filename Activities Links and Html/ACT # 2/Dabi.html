Dabi (My Hero Academia)

Dabi is a character from the anime series My Hero Academia. He is a member of the League of Villains and serves as one of the main antagonists of the series. Dabi is a powerful fire-based villain with a mysterious past and a grudge against the hero society. He is a cunning and ruthless foe, using his powers to manipulate and control others to achieve his goals.
