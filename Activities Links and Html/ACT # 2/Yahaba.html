YAHABA (Demon Slayer)

Yahaba is a demon from the anime series Demon Slayer. He is one of the Twelve Kizuki, holding the position of Lower Moon Six. He is a demon who serves under Muzan Kibutsuji and is tasked with eliminating the Demon Slayer Corps. Yahaba has the ability to manipulate his blood to create illusions and control others. He is a formidable opponent, using his powers to deceive and confuse his enemies.
